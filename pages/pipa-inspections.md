    content: |
      PIPA is an annual inspection scheme for health and safety endorsed by the HSE (Health and Safety Executive). The scheme ensures that inflatable play equipment is regularly inspected, properly maintained, and safe for public use.

      Our inspections follow the EU standard **BS EN 14960**, which sets out the safety requirements and test methods for inflatable play equipment. All operators of inflatable play equipment used for commercial purposes require annual PIPA inspections to comply with health and safety regulations.
